Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 7, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how CityLocal 101 collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website, create an account, or use our services. By using CityLocal 101, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Account information: name, email address, password, and business details you provide.
  • Usage data: pages visited, search queries, and interactions with business listings.
  • Reviews and submissions: ratings, comments, and feedback you post on business profiles.

3. How We Use Your Information

CityLocal 101 uses collected information to:

  • Provide, personalize, and improve the platform experience.
  • Communicate with you about your account, reviews, or support requests.
  • Analyze platform usage to maintain security and improve features.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers who support our operations, and only when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users and community.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

CityLocal 101 uses cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, analyze traffic, and improve performance. You can adjust browser settings to manage cookie usage.

6. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data. While we strive to secure your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Your Choices

You can update or delete your account information at any time by contacting support. You may also opt out of marketing emails by using unsubscribe links within those communications.

8. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@citylocal101.com.
