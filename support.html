Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my business?

Adding your business is FREE and easy! Click "Add Your Business" in the header, fill out the form with your business details, and submit. We'll review and approve it within 24 hours.

How do I write a review?

Click "Write A Review" in the header, search for the business, rate it with stars (1-5), write your experience, and submit. Reviews help other customers make informed decisions.

Is CityLocal 101 free to use?

Yes! CityLocal 101 is completely free for users to browse businesses, read reviews, and write reviews. Business listings are also FREE to add and maintain.

How do I claim my business?

If your business is already listed, contact us at support@citylocal101.com with your business name and proof of ownership. We'll grant you access to manage your listing.

Can I edit or delete my review?

Yes! Log into your account, go to your profile, find the review you want to edit or delete, and make your changes. Review edits are subject to moderation.
